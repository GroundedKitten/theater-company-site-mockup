A Raisin in the Sun

June 1–30, 2024    

Experience the timeless resonance of Lorraine Hansberry’s seminal work, “A Raisin in the Sun,” in its latest revival at Griot Theater. Melody of Me

June 1–30, 2024

June 1–30, 2024    

“Melody of Me” is an inspiring musical journey of self-discovery and resilience. Follow the story of Lily, a bright-eyed girl who, through the power of song and dance, learns to embrace her past and love the person she’s become. This heartwarming tale will lift your spirits and have you humming the tunes of self-love long after the curtains close.

Shadows of Freedom

June 1–30, 2024    

Dive into the heart of the 1960s Civil Rights Movement with “Shadows of Freedom,” a gripping historical drama that follows a group of young activists in their fight for justice and equality. Witness their courage as they navigate the turbulent waters of social change, and discover the unsung heroes who paved the way for a brighter future.

A Walk in Their Shoes

June 1–30, 2024    

Get ready to laugh and think with “A Walk in Their Shoes,” a satirical comedy that turns the tables on prejudice. When a conservative politician and a radio shock jock wake up to find themselves in a world where they’re part of the marginalized group they once opposed, they embark on a hilarious and eye-opening journey to regain their rights—and their identities.
